# TR / Göksel & Kerslake book-coverage audit & gap triage — 2026-07-16

The third registered ledger of the book-coverage-ledger design
(`docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-15-book-coverage-ledger-design.md`; DE/Hammer
was the pilot, ES/B&B the retrofit). An automated `propose:book-coverage` pass
over all 28 G&K chapters proposed one decision per section against the TR
curriculum (67 grammar points at audit time); its GAPs were then triaged
per-section against the chapter text, and every verdict is encoded in
`packages/db/src/curriculum/book-coverage/tr.ts`.

Unlike ES/DE, the TR curriculum currently tops out at **B1** (B2 is a separate
later cycle), so `B2+` is a first-class exclusion reason in this ledger; those
rows are the natural worklist for the future B2 cycle. The 2026-06-20 reverse
audit's conscious deferrals (word order ch. 23, definiteness ch. 22, noun
clauses ch. 24, ki-relatives, imiş) were honoured unless the section text
revealed an A1–B1 slice with an obvious home.

Mirror note: the TR mirror's `index.json` is a flat entry list (no nesting/levels),
unlike ES/DE; the pass used a converted shim (levels derived from section-number
depth) — the vendored TOC snapshot is equivalent in shape to the other ledgers.

## Numbers

- TOC anchors: 583 (28 chapters + front matter, 2 appendices, glossary; 4 excludedSubtree rows cover 7 anchors)

- Triage verdicts: 16 claim (auditor over-flags), 63 fold (existing point widened), 5 new points, 151 exclusions
- All 67 pre-existing TR grammar points were claimed by at least one section
