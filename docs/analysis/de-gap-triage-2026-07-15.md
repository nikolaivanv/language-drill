# DE gap triage — verdicts for the 197 Hammer coverage GAPs (2026-07-15)

Triage of every GAP from `de-hammer-book-coverage-audit-2026-07-15.md` against the
planned 98-point curriculum (`docs/superpowers/plans/2026-07-12-de-a1-b2-curriculum.md`),
following the plan's philosophy: production-error-focused, Menschen/Sicher-driven
level placement, A1–B2 only, no lexical inventories (those belong to the vocab /
dictation follow-up tracks), exercise-generation-friendly.

**Verdict totals: 6 proposed NEW points (absorbing 44 sections) · 42 FOLDs into
existing planned points · 111 EXCLUSIONS with reasons.** Every GAP anchor appears
exactly once below. If the new points are accepted, the plan grows 98 → 104
(A1 18→19, A2 29→31, B1 25→27, B2 26→27).

## A. Proposed NEW points (6)

Plan-style entries, ready to paste into the plan doc's level sections.

1. **`de-a1-numbers-ordinals`** (A1) — Cardinal numbers (compound forms,
   Million/Milliarde, year-reading), eins/ein/einer distribution, ordinals
   -te/-ste (irregular erste/dritte/siebte) + written `8.` notation, dates
   (am 8. Mai / Montag, den 5. Juni), -mal adverbs (einmal, zweimal), decimal
   comma + basic fractions (-el), arithmetic (plus/mal/durch … ist/macht).
   [H §8.1.1–8.1.2, §8.2, §8.3.1, §8.4.3–8.4.4, §8.5.3] — mirrors
   `es-a1-numbers-ordinals` / `tr-a1-numbers-ordinals` precedent.
   *Absorbs GAPs:* `8-1-1`, `8-1-2`, `8-2`, `8-3-1`, `8-4-3`, `8-4-4`, `8-5-3`.

2. **`de-a2-measure-expressions`** (A2) — Singular measure nouns after numerals
   (zwei Pfund, drei Glas Bier), apposition case in measure phrases (eine Tasse
   heißer Kaffee / mit einer Tasse heißem Kaffee — von-paraphrase as fallback),
   halb vs die Hälfte + anderthalb/zweieinhalb, distributive je (je zwei) and
   distributive article (zweimal die Woche, 3 Euro das Kilo), English-plural →
   German-singular measurement divergences. [H §1.2.7–1.2.8, §2.7.1, §8.3.2,
   §8.4.1, §4.7] *Absorbs:* `1-2-7`, `1-2-8`, `2-7-1`, `8-3-2`, `8-4-1` (+ the
   distributive-article half of `4-7`).

3. **`de-a2-quantifiers-other`** (A2) — jeder (full declension), alle,
   viel/viele vs wenig/wenige (declined vs undeclined), ein paar / ein bisschen /
   ein wenig (undeclined; ein paar vs ein Paar), ander- (declension + der andere /
   etwas anderes). [H §5.5.12, §5.5.25, §5.5.5–5.5.6, §5.5.2] — mirrors
   `es-a2-todo-otro-quantifiers`. *Absorbs:* `5-5-2`, `5-5-5`, `5-5-6`,
   `5-5-12`, `5-5-25`.

4. **`de-b1-articles-use`** (B1) — Article use beyond the A1 form paradigms:
   definite article in generalizations (Der Mensch … / Die Tannen …), with
   abstract nouns, substances, meals, diseases, languages and institutions
   (general vs partial sense), with geographical names (die Schweiz/der Iran vs
   neuter zero; mountains/rivers/streets always), definite article required for
   adjective-qualified names (das heutige Deutschland), article instead of
   possessive for attributes/emotions (Er hob die Hand). [H §4.2.1–4.2.2, §4.3,
   §4.4.1, §4.4.3, §4.6.2, §4.7] — mirrors `es-a2-articles-use`. prereq:
   de-a1-zero-article. *Absorbs:* `4-2-1`, `4-2-2`, `4-3`, `4-4-1`, `4-4-3`,
   `4-6-2`, `4-7`.

5. **`de-b1-adjective-case-government`** (B1) — Adjectives taking a dative
   complement (ähnlich, behilflich, dankbar, wichtig, (zu) teuer + person),
   impersonal sensation datives (Mir ist kalt/langweilig/schlecht — never *Ich
   bin kalt), the small accusative set (gewohnt, leid, los, satt, wert), dative
   with zu + adjective (Das ist mir zu teuer). [H §6.3.1–6.3.2, §2.5.4]
   *Absorbs:* `2-5-4`, `6-3-1`, `6-3-2`.

6. **`de-b2-verb-prefixes`** (B2) — Inseparable prefix semantics as productive
   morphology (be- transitivizing, ent- removal, er- achievement/change-of-state,
   ver- completion/wrongly/change, zer- "to pieces"), and the variable prefixes
   with separable-vs-inseparable meaning contrast (durch-, über-, um-, unter-,
   wieder-, wider-, voll-, hinter-, miss-: umfahren vs umfáhren), incl. the
   participle/zu consequences (durchgesetzt vs durchsetzt). [H §20.5, §20.7]
   prereq: de-a2-separable-prefix-verbs, de-a2-past-participle-formation.
   *Absorbs:* `20-5`, `20-5-1`–`20-5-5`, `20-7`, `20-7-1`–`20-7-10`.
