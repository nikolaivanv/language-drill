# DE curriculum source: Hueber *Menschen* (A1–B1) + *Sicher!* (B2) tables of contents

Source material for revising/authoring the DE A1–B2 curriculum, following the
approach established for ES (PRs #528, #529, #532). The Goethe-Institut does
not publish a per-level grammar curriculum, so the Hueber coursebook TOCs
(*Menschen* A1–B1 and its continuation *Sicher!* for B2 — the de-facto
Goethe-exam prep series) serve as the level-placement proxy, analogous to
PCIC for ES and Yedi İklim for TR.

Extraction scope: the **Grammatik** and **Wortfelder** columns per lesson.
Skills columns (Hören/Sprechen/Lesen/Schreiben) and Modul-Plus content are
omitted except where they name a vocab domain not covered by Wortfelder.

## B2 (Sicher!, lessons 1–12)

The *Menschen* series ends at B1; B2 uses Hueber's continuation series
*Sicher!*. Note the format differences: 12 longer lessons instead of 24, and a
per-lesson **Wortschatz** module (one focused topic) instead of *Menschen*'s
Wortfelder list. Grammar entries bundle several items per lesson, including
recurring "Zusammenhänge" units (expressing a logical relation across
subordinate-clause, prepositional, and adverbial variants — verbal vs. nominal
style), which is the characteristic B2 skill.
