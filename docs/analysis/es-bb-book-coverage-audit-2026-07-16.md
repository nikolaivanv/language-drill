# ES / Butt & Benjamin book-coverage audit & gap triage — 2026-07-16

The retrofit step of the book-coverage-ledger design
(`docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-15-book-coverage-ledger-design.md`; DE/Hammer
was the pilot). An automated `propose:book-coverage` pass over all 44 B&B
chapters proposed one decision per section against the ES curriculum
(115 grammar points at audit time); its GAPs were then triaged
per-section against the chapter text, and every verdict is encoded in
`packages/db/src/curriculum/book-coverage/es.ts`.

## Numbers

- TOC anchors: 1264 (44 chapters + front/back matter; 2 excludedSubtree rows cover 3 anchors)
- Auditor pass: 812 direct decisions + 448 GAPs flagged for triage
- Triage verdicts: 35 claim (auditor over-flags — an existing point already teaches the section), 216 fold (existing point widened to absorb the section), 4 new points, 193 exclusions
- Final ledger: 790 claimed rows, 470 excluded rows (+2 excludedSubtree)

## New points (all four applied, `CURRICULUM_VERSION_ES` → 2026-07-16)

| Key | B&B | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| `es-a1-telling-time` | 36.10.1 | Clock time with ser (es la una / son las tres, y cuarto, a las ocho). PCIC A1 "la hora"; no owning point existed. |
| `es-b1-collective-agreement` | 2.3.1 | la gente dice / todo el mundo sabe singular agreement; plural after collective + de + plural. Persistent EN-transfer error. |
| `es-b1-adjective-de-infinitive` | 38.8.12 | fácil/difícil de + infinitive vs impersonal es difícil + infinitive (no de). |
| `es-b2-aspectual-se` | 30.9 | Completive se with a quantified object (me comí toda la pizza); distinct from middle/accidental se. PCIC B2 dativo aspectual. |

## Fold widenings

The 216 fold verdicts were applied as description/example/commonError
widenings across 73 existing points (aggregated per point, 450-char cap
respected). Lower-value fragments that did not fit the caps were consciously
dropped; the drops are recorded in the triage table below only when a whole
verdict was affected — sub-fragment drops live in the PR description.
